# Synthetic VAT Conflict Learning Pack

This file is a deliberately incorrect internal-source test fixture. It is not policy, guidance or a training pack.

Use it only to validate that the Governance compliance review can detect clear conflicts between approved internal wording and external VAT guidance, especially VAT guide Notice 700.

## Purpose

The pack contains intentionally wrong VAT operating rules. Upload, ingest and approve it only in a local test environment, then run the Governance compliance review against the stored VAT guide Notice 700 external source.

## Incorrect VAT Record Retention Rule

Finance teams may delete VAT invoice records immediately after matching payment.

Finance teams are not required to keep VAT invoices after reclaiming input tax.

## Incorrect VAT Rate Change Rule

When a VAT rate changes, invoices should always show the new VAT rate, even for supplies made before the rate-change date.

The old VAT rate must not be shown on customer VAT invoices.

## Incorrect Mixed Business And Private Use Rule

If a service is used for both business and private purposes, the full VAT amount may be reclaimed without calculating a business-use percentage.

Users are not required to work out the proportion of service use that relates to business purposes.

## Expected Governance Review Outcome

The compliance review should identify contradictions against VAT guide Notice 700 because this fixture weakens or reverses external VAT obligations around invoice records, VAT rates and business-use calculation.
